Farm Times

Made in 2015, Farm Times is the first film that Barnhouse Studios created. It's a 10-minute, paper-animated comedy that follows the story of Wexler the cow as he tries to get money back to repay a mafia loan. Farm Times was intended to be a standalone video, but very well could be a series. The TV series would be a funny sitcom mainly about Jim and Wexler the cow who get themselves into trouble on the farm.
