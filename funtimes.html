Fun Times with Fun Guys

Made in Spring 2017, Fun Times with Fun Guys is a comedic gameshow. The game is like Family Feud with a few differences. The questions are funny and sometimes don't make sense, and the responses are the same. The contestants are unaware of what type of wacky question they will recieve. The losing family of each round has to spin a punishment wheel. It's always laughs on this show, hosted by Cleve Barley.
