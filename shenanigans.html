Shenanigans

Shenanigans was started in Spring 2016, and the first episode is likely to be released in Summer 2018. It is an animated series following the shenanigans of best friends Robbie and Hunter. The oblivious duo can typically be found eating terasaki, playing saki-ball, investigating murders, skating, and playing in their band. A normal life at first glance, but it seems like they can't keep themselves out of trouble. Oh yeah, and in this world everyone is an anthropomorphic duck, except for police officers, who are cats.
