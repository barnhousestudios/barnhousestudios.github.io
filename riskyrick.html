Risky Rick (and the Backfield Jump)

Made in Summer 2017, Risky Rick (and the Backfield Jump) is a comedic mockumentary about the notorious Risky Rick in his journey to jumping the backfield jump. Inspiration from his fans motivates him to do the jump, even though it's risky. After all, he is Risky Rick.
